# Weekly self-review checklist

A weekly orientation pass I run on my own work and team.

- How does the capacity landscape of my team look this week?
- Simulate the upcoming 7–14 days: who do I need to inform about what?
- Who should I do a co-working session with this week?
- What fun things can I share with people in the org?
- Am I being open about being overwhelmed / stressed / low energy via meta-schedules?
- Speed-run through all AORs.
- Review to-do list.
