# How do I use aText? (2023-03)

[aText](https://www.trankynam.com/atext/) is a text expander that lets you (a) replace text automatically and (b) search for snippets that then get inserted into the current text field. Useful for:

- Calendar links
- Addresses
- LinkedIn links / frequently-used URLs
- Bank account numbers
- Checklists
- Email templates
- Lots more
