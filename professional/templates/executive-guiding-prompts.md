# Guiding prompts for executives (2023-04)

Three prompts I use to open meetings with senior leadership.

1. Your most important job is making sure your team is kicking ass. What can this group do to make that more likely and consistent?
2. Where is clarity lacking for you on what we're doing, why, and how it's going (at org-wide level)? (Assume any ambiguity you experience is compounded for your people by 5–10×.)
3. Get on the balcony: what is a challenge or problem you foresee that we can either (a) quickly solve here together, or (b) delegate to someone (in this group or on the team)?
