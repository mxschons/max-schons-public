# One-on-one check-in template (2023-04)

A check-in template I use in 1-1s with people I manage.

**Week XX**

1. Brag about at least one professional success of the past week.
2. Current job satisfaction (-10 to 10). Three most relevant variables influencing this right now?
3. What are you procrastinating these days?
4. What level are your stress metrics at?
5. What keeps you away from doing your best work right now? What should you say no to?
6. Who can help you get unstuck on any of the above?
7. Action points of the last meeting(s).
8. Reflect on at least one mistake / challenge over the past week:
   - What exactly happened?
   - Have I made mistakes like this before?
   - What was the immediate cause?
   - What was the root cause? Bonus: is there a function of the root behavior I should be aware of?
   - How can / did I resolve the immediate cause?
   - How can / did I resolve the root cause?
9. What risks and issues do you anticipate for your work and / or performance over the next 1–2 weeks?
   - Risk + mitigation strategy.
   - Do you anticipate personal performance risks / issues?
10. What risks are you currently averse to that could yield unusually high results?
11. Braindump — what's on your mind (questions, asks, tasks, ughs, …)?
    - Self-care (solo time, movement + exercise, life admin, social).
12. What deliberate growth activities & professional-excellence training are you engaged in (maybe KPIs)?
13. Outlook:
    - Immediate (helpful) next steps after this session (what and how)?
    - What should we focus on next time?
    - Was this session helpful (scale -10 to 10)?

**For the manager:** Anything I want to give feedback on, especially performance risks / issues?
