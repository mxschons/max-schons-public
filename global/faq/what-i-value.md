# What I value

- **Bright, beautiful environments.** Light, space, and aesthetics are load-bearing for my wellbeing, not decorative.
- **Shared novel experiences.** Discovery together compounds both the novelty and the connection.
- **Witnessing competence and agency in others.** One of my favourite things.
- **Intellectual stimulation, playfulness, and intimacy as a bundle** — not separate categories to visit in turn.
- **Simplification.** Satisfaction from subtraction, closing loops, elegant systems.
- **Spontaneity within structure.** Optionality built on solid foundations, not in place of them.
- **Sensory richness without over-intellectualising it.**
- **Creating artifacts I can be proud of retrospectively.**
