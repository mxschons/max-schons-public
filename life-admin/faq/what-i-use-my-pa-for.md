# What do I use my PA for? (2023-05)

TLDR:

- Repetitive tasks taking more than 30 min (e.g. merging contacts across systems).
- Iterative research, especially life-admin topics.

A nice side effect: you have to articulate what you actually want. For example, having written down [my preferences](../preferences.md) for accommodation and food, and [my travel preferences](../../travel/preferences.md), my PA can make better decisions on my behalf. The more articulated my preferences get, the more useful my PA becomes.
