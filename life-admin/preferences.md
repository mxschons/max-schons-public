# Preferences

How I like things when someone is helping me with logistics — accommodation, travel, food. Personal to me; adapt to your own life.

---

## Accommodation

### Long-stay preferences

**Surroundings:**
- Green areas close by.
- Public transport close by.
- Good supermarkets close by.
- Supermarket delivery available.
- Variety of vegan food delivery available.
- Ideally a gym within walking distance; bouldering hall within ~10-min bike ride.
- Outer parts of the city are fine as long as I get to the center in < 45 min door-to-door on public transport without switching trams/buses.

**Must-haves:**
- Excellent internet.
- Calm / silent surroundings.
- A lot of light.
- Kitchen installed.
- No "12 months mandatory stay" clause.

**Great-to-have:**
- Bath tub.
- Chill neighbors.
- Plants in the apartment.
- Large mirror(s).

**Size / format:**
- 35–55 m² studio ideally, or a similar one-room apartment.
- Alternative: ~20–30 m² room in a shared flat with 3–4 people.
- No group houses.
- Furnished or unfurnished both fine.
- Budget target: ~50€/day, up to 150€.
- Ability to sublet while away for longer is a plus.

### Preferred specifics (updated 2023-08-14)

- At least one room ≥ 25 m²; ideally wall height > 3.5 m.
- Kitchen or kitchenette (mini oven is great).
- Within ~5 min walking distance, ideally in the same building: 24/7 gym, co-working, laundry, trees/green space.
- Within ~10 min walking distance: groceries.
- A lot of light in the room (or co-working area): big windows, floor-to-ceiling preferred, ideally two window fronts — > 500 lux, ideally 1,000–5,000. ([Examples](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bkjZJEnuG-s&ab_channel=30X40DesignWorkshop))
- Indirect light is nicer than direct light in almost all cases.
- AC if hot climate.
- High-speed internet.
- Desk to work at.
- Quiet environment.
- Ideally: two rooms (one for sleep, one for work); on a higher floor (5th+, the higher the better); door that unlocks with a code or phone.
- Drying rack or dryer.
- A big table close to a window.
- Big fridge + freezer (less frequent shopping trips).
- Carpets cozy enough you want to sit on them.
- Open closets for circulation.
- Low-maintenance plants.
- Great ventilation.
- Not messy.
- Online/app services available: vegan food ordering, taxis, grocery delivery.
- **Bonus:** dishwasher; view-protected sun balcony.

## Travel

### Things to do before travelling somewhere

- Get a [Nomad List](https://nomadlist.com/) summary of the place.
- Sanity-check the [German Foreign Ministry website](https://www.auswaertiges-amt.de/de/ReiseUndSicherheit/reise-und-sicherheitshinweise) for special regulations.

### New-country checklist

- Check the foreign-ministry situation assessment immediately.
- Install an eSIM.
- Get travel insurance.
- Check passport validity.
- Check additional vaccinations.
- Check weather forecast.
- Ensure I know how to access my emergency documents.
- Familiarise myself with local emergency numbers.

### New-in-a-city checklist

**Food:**
- Order [Huel](https://de.huel.com/) or Soylent to the place.
  - ~1 month: Huel Black Edition in Vanilla and Cookie & Cream.
  - < 1 month: mix Ready-to-Drink, 1 set / 10 days.
- Have a list of 5 great vegan restaurants nearby to check out.
- Get a list of 5 great vegan delivery services.

**Exercise:**
- Identify a gym within < 5 min walk.
- Know where the closest sauna is.
- Know where the closest bouldering hall is.

**Work:**
- Have a list of co-working spaces with phone booths; check for standing desks, ideally walking pads.

**Social:**
- Check local effective-altruism events.
- Get a list of really cool TripAdvisor activities.

**On arrival, get:**
- Nuts.
- Gum.
- Pepper spray.
- Sunscreen.
- Find a gym.
- Maybe flowers.

### Travel preferences

I usually schedule travel myself but occasionally ask for support. Preferences:

- I'd rather pay a bit more / travel a bit longer for simplicity of itinerary.
- **Trains:**
  - Always the best connection, irrespective of price.
  - Second class preferred; first class is fine if second is full.
  - Fewest transfers. If it takes up to 20% longer but avoids switching (so I can keep focused work going), prefer the longer direct route.
- **Flights:**
  - I usually schedule flights myself.
  - Check in with me before booking anything other than economy.
  - Non-stop or 1-stop strongly preferred; depending on price craziness, ask me before booking non-stop.
