## Accommodation

### Long-stay preferences

**Surroundings:**
- Green areas close by.
- Public transport close by.
- Good supermarkets close by.
- Supermarket delivery available.
- Variety of vegan food delivery available.
- Ideally a gym within walking distance; bouldering hall within ~10-min bike ride.
- Outer parts of the city are fine as long as I get to the center in < 45 min door-to-door on public transport without switching trams/buses.

**Must-haves:**
- Excellent internet.
- Calm / silent surroundings.
- A lot of light.
- Kitchen installed.
- No "12 months mandatory stay" clause.

**Great-to-have:**
- Bath tub.
- Chill neighbors.
- Plants in the apartment.
- Large mirror(s).

**Size / format:**
- 35–55 m² studio ideally, or a similar one-room apartment.
- Alternative: ~20–30 m² room in a shared flat with 3–4 people.
- No group houses.
- Furnished or unfurnished both fine.
- Budget target: ~50€/day, up to 150€.
- Ability to sublet while away for longer is a plus.

### Preferred specifics (updated 2023-08-14)

- At least one room ≥ 25 m²; ideally wall height > 3.5 m.
- Kitchen or kitchenette (mini oven is great).
- Within ~5 min walking distance, ideally in the same building: 24/7 gym, co-working, laundry, trees/green space.
- Within ~10 min walking distance: groceries.
- A lot of light in the room (or co-working area): big windows, floor-to-ceiling preferred, ideally two window fronts — > 500 lux, ideally 1,000–5,000. ([Examples](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bkjZJEnuG-s&ab_channel=30X40DesignWorkshop))
- Indirect light is nicer than direct light in almost all cases.
- AC if hot climate.
- High-speed internet.
- Desk to work at.
- Quiet environment.
- Ideally: two rooms (one for sleep, one for work); on a higher floor (5th+, the higher the better); door that unlocks with a code or phone.
- Drying rack or dryer.
- A big table close to a window.
- Big fridge + freezer (less frequent shopping trips).
- Carpets cozy enough you want to sit on them.
- Open closets for circulation.
- Low-maintenance plants.
- Great ventilation.
- Not messy.
- Online/app services available: vegan food ordering, taxis, grocery delivery.
- **Bonus:** dishwasher; view-protected sun balcony.

### Examples of places I liked

- [Wunderlocke (Munich)](https://www.lockeliving.com/en/munich/wunderlocke/living/micro-studio) — decent price, very nice environment, clean, co-working area, close to a 24/7 gym, aparthotel suited for longer stays.
- [Heri Collective (Singapore, Arab St.)](https://hericoll.com/collections/studio-arab-st/).

## Food plan

If I prepare food for myself, this is roughly what it looks like.

### Breakfast: nut / seeds / whole grains / omega-3 / berry / spice

| Ingredient | Amount per week |
|------------|-----------------|
| Unsalted mixed nuts and seeds | 250 g |
| Chia, hemp, or flax seeds (ground) | 100 g |
| Small amount of mixed whole grains, unsweetened | 300 g |
| Frozen berries | 400 g |
| Soy yogurt, ideally skyr | 1.5 kg |
| Vegan protein powder | 7 servings |
| Cinnamon or similar | 1–2 g / day |

### Lunch: leafy greens + mixed veggies + vegan meat (+ fermented something) + herbs

| Ingredient | Amount per week |
|------------|-----------------|
| Frozen mixed veggies without dressing | 7 × 250 g (or one bigger pack) |
| Packed mixed salad (much leafy green, little lettuce) | 7 × 75 g |
| Bundles of herbs (mostly from Turkish supermarkets) | 7 × 20 g |
| Fermented veggies (sauerkraut, kimchi, tempeh) | 7 × 50 g |
| Sprouts | 100–150 g |
| Vegan meat alternative | 7 × ~200 g package |
| Avocado | 5–7 |

### Lentil / peas + root vegetables soup + herbs

| Ingredient | Amount per week |
|------------|-----------------|
| Lentils / peas | 0.5 kg (or 3 cans) |
| Root vegetables (frozen) | 1 kg |
| Soup spice mix | 7 servings |
| Huel / other meal replacement | 7 servings |
| Spice-mix paste with little or no sugar | ~20 g / day |

### Snacks

| Ingredient | Amount per week |
|------------|-----------------|
| Fruit | 7 servings |
| 90%+ dark chocolate | 1 × ~100 g |
| Non-peanut nut butter without salt / sugar | 100 g |

### Miscellaneous

| Ingredient | Amount per week |
|------------|-----------------|
| Unsweetened plant milk | 1 L |

### Dessert

Dark chocolate or nut butter.

### Post-workout drink

Protein powder + fruit + nut milk.

Good food-plan subscription services that send ingredients approximating the above are awesome.
