# Media preferences

## Film & TV

**Like:** stylish action/crime, clever thrillers (not psychological), dark humour, smart character-driven sci-fi, music-centric stories or standout scores, feel-good ensemble comedy, international auteur work (KR/JP especially), auteur animation.

**Dislike:** psychological horror, bleak thrillers, long high-fantasy epics, kid-centric sentimental animation.

**Tone signals:** I want real laughs, sharp dialogue. War or history only if top-tier. Superhero selectively.

**Anchor examples — like:** Bullet Train, The Godfather, Goodfellas, The Departed, Inception, Fight Club, Parasite (2019), Spirited Away, Whiplash, Inglourious Basterds, Joker (2019), Schindler's List, Severance, Ted Lasso, Dark Matter.

**Anchor examples — mixed:** Saving Private Ryan, The Lord of the Rings, WALL-E.

## Music

**Core rotation:** hip-hop (US and German, lyrical swagger), pop/R&B with strong hooks, electronic/EDM, film scores (Justin Hurwitz, Nicholas Britell, Rob Simonsen), classical leaning Bach — piano and violin — plus Mozart, Schubert, Elgar, Ghibli/Hisaishi. Novelty and comedy music is fair game.
